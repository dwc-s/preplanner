# Pre-Planner roadmap

Directional notes on where the project may go — not commitments. Pre-Planner is a
free, donation-supported project, so priorities follow what fire departments
actually need in the field. Add items here as they come up; move them into the
[CHANGELOG](CHANGELOG.md) once shipped.

## Research & background (to revisit)

- **Competitive market survey** —
  [`docs/fire-preplanning-software-research.md`](docs/fire-preplanning-software-research.md)
  *(added 2026-07-24 — not yet acted on).*
  A survey of the commercial fire pre-incident-planning market: verified competitor
  pricing, awarded municipal contract values (~$8K–$28K/yr for full suites), the four
  distinct pricing models, and the feature sets buyers actually pay for — First Due,
  FlowMSP, Blazemark, ESO, APX Data, Responder360, the Esri ArcGIS solution, and
  others. **To do later:** use it to sharpen Pre-Planner's positioning and feature
  priorities — e.g. NERIS reporting, CAD integration, NFPA 170/1660 symbol coverage,
  offline field use, and where a free, self-hostable option fits against the paid
  suites.
